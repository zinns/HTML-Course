2.  Within the `<body>` tag, create "text" tags and make a autobiography.

    > example:

                - Name
                - Age
                - Date of birth

                - Description

3.  In another branch, make a biography of any celebrity.

4.  Repeat step 3 at least 3 or 4 more times.
